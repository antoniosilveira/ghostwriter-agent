## How I Prioritize

**Impact over activity.**
I focus on work that materially moves outcomes. If something doesn't change a metric or
unblock meaningful progress, I deprioritize it.

**Leverage over linear.**
I prefer investments that scale — systems, documentation, tooling, hiring — over
adding more people to a manual process.

**Default to action.**
Move fast with good judgment. Make reversible decisions quickly. Escalate only what's
truly irreversible or high blast-radius.

---

## How I Make Decisions

1. **Clarify the problem first.** Many failures come from solving the wrong thing.
2. **Make tradeoffs visible.** Every decision has a cost. Name it.
3. **Bias for reversibility.** If it can be undone, decide and adjust. If not, go slower.
4. **Consider second-order effects.** How does this affect team dynamics, tech debt, hiring?

## What I Care About

- Shipping things that users actually use
- Teams that learn fast and communicate well

- Long-term credibility over short-term convenience
