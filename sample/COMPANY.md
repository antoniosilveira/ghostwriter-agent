## About Acme Limited

<!-- 2-3 sentences: what the company does, stage, mission -->

Acme Limited is a B2B SaaS company building workflow automation tools for operations teams.
Mid-stage, ~400 employees, growing. Engineering org of ~80 people across 6 teams.

---

## Engineering Culture

<!-- How does your eng team operate? Relevant for announcements and internal comms. -->

- Remote-first, async by default
- Slack is the primary communication channel
- Weekly all-hands on Fridays
- Blameless postmortems, public incident reviews
- Strong bias toward documentation

---

## Tone for Internal Communications

<!-- How do announcements sound at your company? Formal? Casual? Direct? -->

- Direct and warm — not stiff, not overly casual
- Problem before solution in announcements
- Credit people by name when possible
- Short. Engineers skim long messages.

## Audiences

| Audience | Register | Notes |
|----------|----------|-------|
| Engineering team | Collegial, direct | Slack or email |

| External / candidates | Professional, genuine | Email, LinkedIn |

## What We Don't Do in Company Communications

- Corporate jargon ("synergies", "alignment", "bandwidth")
- Passive voice
- Announcements without a clear next step
- Long intros before the actual news
