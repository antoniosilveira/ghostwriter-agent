## How We Work

**Rhythm:**
- Weekly all-hands every Friday at 10am PT (Zoom + recorded)
- Monthly company retrospective: last Thursday of the month
- Quarterly planning: first week of each quarter
- Annual offsite: September

**Communication defaults:**
- Slack is the primary async channel
- Email for external and formal communication
- Zoom for meetings that need real-time discussion
- Loom for async walkthroughs that don't need a live meeting

**Decision-making:**
- Small, reversible decisions: make them and communicate
- Large or irreversible decisions: write a brief doc, get input, then decide
- Cross-functional decisions: involve the right people early, not at the end

**Engineering processes:**
- Two-week sprints, planning on Mondays
- Blameless postmortems for all SEV-1 and SEV-2 incidents
- RFC process for architectural decisions
- Code review required for all production changes

## Tone for Internal Communications

**Slack announcements:**
- Direct and warm — not stiff, not overly casual
- Problem before solution
- Credit people by name when possible
- Short: engineers skim long messages
- Use @here for things the team needs to act on, @channel only for truly urgent items

**All-company emails:**
- More structured than Slack
- Include a clear subject line
- Open with the most important information, not background
- Close with a concrete action or next step

**What we don't do:**
- Corporate jargon (synergies, alignment, bandwidth, leverage)
- Passive voice
- Long intros before the actual point
- Announcements without a clear next step
