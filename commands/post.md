# /post

**Purpose:** Draft a short LinkedIn post or social post.

**Input:** Topic, context, or content to post about. Can be:
- A topic: "posting about our new async-first engineering culture"
- A repost: "reposting Sarah's article on distributed teams" + paste the article or link
- A reaction: "want to post about this person's work"

**Workflow:**
1. Identify post type:
   - Original post: sharing a perspective or update
   - Repost with comment: amplifying someone else's work
   - Congratulatory: celebrating a team member or colleague
2. Draft using VOICE.md LinkedIn patterns:
   - Character statement or specific observation first — not job title, not generic praise
   - Short: 2-4 sentences for repost comments, up to 6-8 for original posts
   - Close with pride or a forward-looking statement, not hype
   - One emoji max, at the end, if it fits naturally
3. No hashtag suggestions unless specifically asked

**Output format:**
```
[draft]
```

**Constraints:**
- No em dashes
- No AI vocabulary: crucial, foster, ensure, leverage, seamless, etc.
- Never open with "I'm excited to share" or "Thrilled to announce"
- Repost comments must add a point of view — not just "Great read"
